01 / PROSPECTING
Daily Prospecting System
How to build and work your warm market every single day
1
Build Your Names List

Write down 100 people you know. Friends, family, neighbors, coworkers, church members, social media contacts. Everyone goes on the list first.

2
Daily Activity Goal

Minimum 5 contacts per day. Reach out by call, text, or social message. The goal is conversations, not appointments yet.

3
Set the Appointment

Use the approved Prevail script to set a 30-minute family protection review. You are not selling on the phone. You are scheduling a conversation.

4
Track Everything

Log every contact, every attempt, every appointment in your tracking sheet. Numbers do not lie. Your activity tells you where you will be in 30 days.

5
Expand Beyond Warm Market

Once your warm market is exhausted, move to referrals. Ask every appointment for two names. The pipeline never stops if you work the system.

6
Weekly Review with Leader

Every week your numbers go to your Prevail leader for coaching. Activity is reviewed, accountability is built, and your next week gets sharper.

📊
02 / PRESENTATION
The Family Protection Review
A proven appointment structure that builds trust and closes business
1
Fact-Finding

Spend the first 10 minutes asking questions. What do they have in place? What are their goals? What worries them about their family's future?

2
Present the Solution

Walk through the Field Training presentation based on their specific situation. Tailor the product to their need, not a generic pitch.

3
Handle Objections

Use the approved objection-handling scripts. The most common objections are covered in training. Confidence comes from repetition.

4
Close and Submit

Complete the application in the appointment when possible. Same-day submissions close at a significantly higher rate than callbacks.

📞
03 / FOLLOW-UP
Follow-Up Framework
How to stay top of mind without being pushy
1
Same-Day Thank You

Send a text or message within 2 hours of every appointment. Thank them for their time. Confirm any next steps discussed.

2
72-Hour Check-In

For pending applications or unresolved conversations, follow up within 3 days. Keep it brief and value-driven, not desperate.

3
30-Day Policy Delivery

When a policy is issued, deliver it personally when possible. This is also when you ask for referrals. Satisfied clients are your best source of new business.

4
Annual Review

Put every client on an annual review cycle. Life changes. Coverage needs change. A simple review call deepens trust and often opens new business.

🤝
04 / RECRUITING
Building Your Team
How to identify, recruit, and bring people into the Prevail system
1
Identify Prospects

Look for people with drive, people skills, and a desire for more. The best recruits often already know you. People join people before they join companies.

2
Set the Appointment

Invite them to an appointment. Do not try to fully explain the opportunity in a text or a casual conversation. Let the appointment do the work.

3
Run the B.O.M.

Bring serious prospects to a Business Opportunity Meeting. Group exposure is more powerful than individual explanation. Let them see the room.

4
Support the Fast Start

Once licensed, hold a Fast Start session with your new recruit within 7 days. Speed of launch determines success rate. Do not let momentum die.

🏆
05 / LEADERSHIP
The Leadership Pipeline
The path from new agent to Marketing Director and beyond
1
Agent

Licensed. Running appointments. Building a book of business. Focused on production and mastering the presentation system.

2
Senior Agent

Consistent production. Beginning to recruit and support newer agents. Starting to develop leadership habits and mentorship skills.

3
Marketing Director

Running a personal team. Overriding on team production. Operating as a leader who coaches, recruits, and drives team results independently.

4
Senior Marketing Director and Beyond

Building a regional agency. Developing other Marketing Directors. Creating residual income through team overrides at multiple levels.
