Step by Step

HOW TO GET
LICENSED

01

Book Your Recruiting Interview (R.I.)

Your first step is a 45-minute R.I. with one of our licensed brokers. This is where you learn about the opportunity, ask questions, and decide if Prevail is the right fit for you.

02

Complete Your Pre-Licensing Education

Register for your state's pre-licensing insurance course. This is typically 20 to 40 hours of self-paced online content. We'll guide you to the right provider.

03

Pass Your State Licensing Exam

Schedule and pass your state's life insurance exam. Our team provides study resources and support to help you pass on your first attempt.

04

Submit Your License Application

Apply for your official state license through the relevant regulatory body. We'll walk you through the paperwork and background check requirements.

05

Get Contracted with PHP Agency Carriers

Once licensed, get contracted with PHP's top carriers: National Life Group, AIG, Mutual of Omaha, North American, and more. You'll have access to products that protect families nationwide.

06

Book Your Fast Start Session

Your 1-hour Fast Start session is your official onboarding into the Prevail system. We cover your first appointment script, your pipeline, and your 90-day game plan.
