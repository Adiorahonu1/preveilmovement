PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: April 21, 2026

The Prevail Movement ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services.

Information We Collect

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:

  • Create an account on our website
  • Book an appointment or consultation
  • Subscribe to our email updates
  • Contact us through our website or phone

This information may include:

  • Name: your full name as provided during registration
  • Email address: used for account access, communications, and updates
  • Phone number: used for appointment reminders, account notifications, and text message communications
  • Account credentials: email and password used to access your agent portal

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide access to our agent portal, training materials, and resources
  • Send appointment reminders and confirmations
  • Communicate important updates about your account or our services
  • Send text messages (SMS) related to your account, appointments, and the Prevail Movement
  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests
  • Improve our website and services

Text Message (SMS) Communications

If you provide your phone number and consent to receive text messages, the following terms apply:

  • Message content: You may receive text messages related to your account, appointment reminders, training updates, and information about The Prevail Movement.
  • Message frequency: Message frequency varies based on your activity and account status.
  • Opt out: You can opt out of text messages at any time by replying STOP to any message you receive from us.
  • Help: For assistance, reply HELP to any text message or call us at (575) 571-7057.
  • Costs: Message and data rates may apply. Check with your mobile carrier for details about your messaging plan.
  • Supported carriers: Our messaging services support all major U.S. carriers including AT&T, Verizon, T-Mobile, Sprint, and others.

Your consent to receive text messages is not a condition of purchasing any goods or services. You may opt out at any time without affecting your account access.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes. We may share your information with:

  • Service providers: Trusted third parties that help us operate our website and deliver services (e.g., hosting, authentication, messaging platforms). These providers are contractually obligated to protect your information.
  • Legal compliance: When required by law, regulation, or legal process.
  • Business transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

Data Storage and Security

Your information is stored securely using industry standard encryption and security practices. Account data is protected through our authentication provider with encrypted connections. While no method of electronic storage is 100% secure, we implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information.

Cookies and Analytics

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to improve your browsing experience and analyze website traffic. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request that we correct inaccurate information.
  • Deletion: Request that we delete your personal information, subject to legal retention requirements.
  • Opt out of SMS: Reply STOP to any text message at any time.
  • Opt out of email: Use the unsubscribe link in any marketing email.

To exercise these rights, contact us at (575) 571-7057 or through our website.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a minor, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Your continued use of our website after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your personal information, please contact us:

  • Phone: (575) 571-7057
  • Website: Book a Call
